Digital Experience Monitoring (DEM) is essential for understanding how users interact with your applications—from failed payments and slow user journeys to crashes and stability regressions. At the same time, DEM often intersects with personal or other sensitive data, a risk that industries such as banking, retail, and healthcare are especially sensitive to. To drive adoption across teams, organizations need confidence that DEM can deliver critical insights without compromising on privacy or compliance.
